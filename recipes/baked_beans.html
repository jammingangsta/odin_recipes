Baked Beans

Description

Baked beans is a dish traditionally containing white common beans that are parboiled and then baked in sauce at low temperature for a lengthy period.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of baked beans
  • 1/2 cup of diced onions
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a baking dish, combine the baked beans, diced onions, brown sugar, mustard, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stir well to mix all ingredients.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes or until heated through and bubbly.
